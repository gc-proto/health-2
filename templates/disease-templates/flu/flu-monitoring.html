FluWatch monitoring

Weekly FluWatch report

FluWatch reports, posted every Friday, contain specific information for health professionals on flu viruses circulating in Canada.

Check weekly influenza report

FluWatch is Canada's national surveillance system that monitors the spread of flu and flu-like illnesses on an on-going basis.

  • All FluWatch monitoring

    • Become a FluWatcher

    FluWatchers is an online health surveillance system. During flu season, from October to May, you will get a weekly e-mail. It will ask you 2 questions to find out if you had a cough or fever.

    It’s fast and simple. You only need 15 seconds a week and your answers are anonymous. We follow the rules of the Privacy Act with respect to the information that we collect from you.

    Invite your friends, family and colleagues to join. You can provide valuable information that improves public health in your community and across the country.
